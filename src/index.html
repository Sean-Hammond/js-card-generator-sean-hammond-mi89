Every card is random. You're not limited to a single deck! However, you can only see the topmost card in each pile. And when you pick it up -- the card underneath will also be completely random!

So be careful, because if you pick up one of the cards in the "foundations" piles (the piles that start with aces), you might lose your progress!

Otherwise, normal Solitaire rules for stacking apply. Put the next highest card in order of the same suit on top of a foundations pile. Put the next lowest card of the opposite color on top of one of the "tableau" piles (the lower row of piles). Move cards between piles, but remember that the one underneath will be random.

How to move a card: Select a card and it will have a red border around it. Select the card you want to move it on top of, and it will move there if it can.

Point system: You start with 10 points. Minus 1 every time you turn over the next pile in the deck. Plus 5 every time you make a correct move.
